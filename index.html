Member of Technical Staff · xAI

Zhuqi Li

My focus is multimodal reinforcement learning and high-performance inference for intelligent agents—especially computer-use agents and the Imagine multimodal agent (including reward modeling and RL training).

At xAI, I work on real-time agent serving and multimodal RL systems. Previously, I was a Senior Research Scientist at ByteDance, building production LLM / VLM inference on xLLM and post-training infrastructure at scale.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Princeton University, advised by Professor Kyle Jamieson, and my B.S. (Summa Cum Laude) from Peking University.
