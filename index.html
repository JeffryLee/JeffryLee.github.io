About

Systems for intelligent agents at scale

I am a Member of Technical Staff at xAI, working on LLM inference for computer-use agents and reinforcement learning for Imagine agents, including reward modeling.

Previously, I was a Senior Research Scientist at ByteDance, where I built production LLM / VLM inference on xLLM and post-training infrastructure with verl—shipping models such as Qwen, Seed, DeepSeek-R1, and Wan across thousands of GPUs.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Princeton University, advised by Professor Kyle Jamieson. My earlier research spanned cross-layer video delivery, wireless systems, and large-scale video analytics—work published at SIGCOMM, NSDI, and related venues.
